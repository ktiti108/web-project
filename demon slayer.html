Story of the movie

The Infinity Castle arc in Demon Slayer is the final, epic battle where the Demon Slayer Corps, led by Tanjiro and the Hashira, are trapped in Muzan Kibutsuji's shifting stronghold to defeat him and end demonkind, featuring intense, personal fights against Upper Rank demons like Doma, Akaza, and Kokushibo, complex backstories (especially for Akaza/Hakuji), and high stakes as slayers confront their pasts and fight for humanity's survival in a non-stop, maze-like dimension.
